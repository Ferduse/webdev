Tiger

Tigers are native to India and other parts of southeast Asia. They are mammals and their diet consits of deers and antelope which means they are carnivores. Their average weight is around 450 pounds. Tigers usually hunts in the dark to kill their prey unexpectedly.

They are known for their reddish-orange and black stripes. Their fur helps them blend in to their enviorment when they are chasing down a prey.

They are also very cute.
